Quantum Visuals

Site Name

Quantum Visuals

This name represents a modern and professional photography brand specializing in portrait and event photography. It conveys clarity, precision, and creativity—perfect for capturing special moments like weddings and outdoor shoots.

Site Purpose

Quantum Visuals showcases a curated portfolio of portrait photography, with a focus on weddings, outdoor events, and personal portraits. The website serves as a platform for potential clients to explore my work, learn about my style, and easily get in touch for bookings and inquiries.

Scenarios

  • A couple planning their wedding wants to see examples of previous wedding photography to decide if Quantum Visuals is the right photographer
  • A family wants to book an outdoor portrait session and needs to find contact details and available services

Color Schema

  • Soft ivory (#f8f4f0) — background color, creating a clean and elegant canvas for the photos
  • Deep charcoal (#2e2e2e) — used for headings and text to ensure readability and sophistication

Typography

  • Headings: Playfair Display — elegant serif font to add style and professionalism to titles
  • Body Text: Open Sans — clean, sans-serif font for easy reading of descriptions and information

Wireframes

Mobile View: Vertical layout with Quantum Visuals logo and name at the top, an introductory text, a scrollable gallery showcasing highlights, and a contact button fixed at the bottom for easy access.

Desktop View: Horizontal layout featuring a top navigation bar, a left sidebar with photographer bio and contact info, a main section displaying the gallery grid, and a footer with social media links and additional contact details.
